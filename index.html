Overview

This two-year structured program includes both undergraduate and graduate-level mathematics coursework, alongside an integrated reading plan focused on key thinkers Freud, Marx, Darwin, Cantor, and Dostoevsky. The primary goal is the submission of a comprehensive 60-page paper analyzing Freud in his historical scientific context by July of Year 2.

Learning Strategies

Managing focus and productivity with ADHD requires adaptive strategies. Here are tailored approaches to ensure effective learning and consistent progress:

  • Structured Routine: Maintain a consistent daily schedule with breaks to manage focus.
  • Chunked Study Sessions: Use short, focused intervals (25-30 minutes) with breaks (Pomodoro Technique).
  • Active Learning: Engage through teaching, summarizing, or creating visual aids.
  • Task Variation: Switch between types of activities to maintain engagement.
  • Environmental Control: Minimize distractions with a dedicated study space.
  • External Motivation: Regular check-ins and productivity apps like Notion.
  • Mindfulness and Movement: Short exercises to reset focus.
